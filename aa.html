Smart Investment

Why Invest in Mysore Real Estate

Backed by data and driven by Mysore's location advantage and infrastructure development.

01

Long Term Gains

Mysore offers a salubrious climate and a very peaceful atmosphere with amenities of a big city. Ideal for both investment and self-living.

02

Tax Benefits

Real estate investors get tax exemption from owning rental property under Section 24 of the Income Tax Act. A major reason to invest.

03

Counter-balance Inflation

Real estate acts as a hedge against inflation. With high inflation, rental income and property value increase significantly.

04

Cost Effective

Mysore is a tier-2 city where demand is increasing but property prices are still affordable. A lucrative option for high-end returns.
